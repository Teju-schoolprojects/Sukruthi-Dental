# Design brief: Sukruthi Dental and Oral Care
## Design read
For patients in Mysore seeking trusted, high-end oral healthcare, delivered through a serene, bright clinical aesthetic that alleviates medical anxiety.

## Concept spine
A day at Sukruthi: tracing a patient's transition from initial visit and deep care to the confidence of a healthy, bright smile.

## Locked palette
- Ground: `#F7F9F8`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Text ink: `#10221F`
- Muted text: `#5C6F6B`
- ACCENT: `#0E6E73`

Defense: This clean, Porcelain Daylight palette references sterilizing environments and natural daylight, evoking absolute safety, clinical hygiene, and fresh breath wellness.

## Locked type
- Display: Cabinet Grotesk (500/700/800)
- Body: Inter Tight (400)
No third font.

## Hero technique
A: aperture mask reveal. The central film of the clinic opening up transitions from a contained arch to full-bleed, mirroring the expansion of a confident smile.

## Section plan
1. Hero - pinned film A
2. Proof strip - facts + hairline dividers
3. Space chapter - sticky clinic walk
4. Journey - steps 01-04 checkup process
5. Offerings - click-to-expand rows
6. Technology - split copy/film
7. Protocol band - safety wash band
8. Team - specialty rows
9. Niche band - implants icon focus
10. Care - offset pediatric layout
11. Quote - oversized patient statement
12. Promises - 3 clinic promises
13. Feature portrait - tall 3:4 whitening focus
14. Urgency band - solid accent CTA band
15. FAQ - plain accordion
16. Legacy statement - centered manifesto
17. Checklist - preparation wash rows
18. Visit finale - full-bleed contact card (id="visit")

## CTA inventory
- Primary Action: Slide-up-reveal block CTA (label: "Secure Your Slot")
- Secondary Action: Traveling-arrow text link (label: "Learn Treatment Details")
- Quick Emergency CTA: Oversized phone button (label: "Call Clinic Helpdesk")
